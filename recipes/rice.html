Rice

Rice, a versatile staple, embodies simplicity and sustenance. Its plump grains, humble yet elegant, offer a canvas for culinary creativity. From sushi to fragrant curries, rice seamlessly weaves into diverse cuisines, exuding comfort and tradition in every bite.

This recipe will give you the simplist way to cook rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put 1 cup of rice into the rice cooker
  2. Put 1 cup of water into the rice cooker
  3. Close the lid and press cook
  4. Wait for the rice cooker to do the hard work
  5. Enjoy your cooked rice!
