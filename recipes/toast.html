Toast

Toast is a simple joy. Bread turns golden and crunchy after some heat, ready for all sorts of toppings from butter and jam to avocado or savory spreads. Its warm, familiar taste makes it perfect for breakfast or a quick, satisfying snack.

This recipe will provide the easiest way to cook toast

Ingredients

  • Bread

Steps

  1. Put bread in to a toaster
  2. Turn toaster on
  3. Wait until toaster finishes cooking
  4. Enjoy your toast
