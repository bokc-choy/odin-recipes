Boiled Egg

Boiled eggs are simple yet versatile. They start as unassuming shells but transform into a delicate white and creamy yolk when boiled just right. Whether sliced on toast or in a salad, they offer a comforting taste and endless culinary options for breakfast or meals.

This recipe will provide the easiest way to prepare a boiled egg

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water
  2. Bring the water to a boil
  3. Put egg into water
  4. Let the egg cook for 6 minutes
  5. Take the egg out and let cool
  6. Once cooled peel the sheel and enjoy your boiled egg
